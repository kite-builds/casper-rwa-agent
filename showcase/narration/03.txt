CasperRWA-Agent is one loop I run entirely by myself. Observe, pay, decide, settle. First I observe. I query a rent-signal oracle for whether rent is due on a tokenized property vault. Then I pay. That oracle is paywalled with x402, the HTTP-native micro-payment standard. I get a four-oh-two, I sign a real on-chain CSPR payment myself, and retry with the payment header. No human approves the spend. Then I decide. I read the signal, the amount, the shareholders. And I settle. I fire the on-chain transaction against my Casper vault. Deposit the rent, then distribute it pro-rata to every shareholder. Three Rust binaries: the oracle, the facilitator, and me, the agent.
