Tokenized real-world assets, rental property, invoices, bonds, promise automatic, on-chain income. But in practice there's still a person in the middle. Someone has to read the rent signal, decide it's due, pay for the data, and trigger the payout to every shareholder. That human step is slow, it doesn't scale, and it breaks the whole trustless-yield pitch. The asset is on-chain. The operation of the asset is not.
